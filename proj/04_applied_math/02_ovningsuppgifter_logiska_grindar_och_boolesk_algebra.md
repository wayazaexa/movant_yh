a) $22 >> 1. 22 = 00010110_2 \rightarrow 00001011_2 = 11$

__7. Förenkla uttryck__

$$
X + X = X \\
X \cdot X = X \\
X + X' = 1 \\
X \cdot X' = 0 \\
X + 1 = 1 \\
X \cdot 0 = 0 \\
X + 0 = X \\
X \cdot 1 = X \\
(X')' = X
$$
